Guide to raise your first cats

1. Consider whether there are physical and environmental conditions for cats.

Expenses: Taking safe commercial cat dry food as the minimum standard, the monthly cost of cat food + cat litter + necessary medicines should be about 100~150 yuan.

Basic environmental assessment for cat raising: whether there is a cat litter box that can be isolated from food and water sources, preferably from where you sleep. And it can meet the space for cats to run and climb. If you don't have such conditions, it may be torture for the cat and for you, and it is difficult to last.

2. Obtain the consent of those around you who may be affected, such as landlords, roommates, family members, etc.

3. Small animals need companionship. Although cats are relatively independent, they are prone to depression when they are often left alone. Please spend at least half an hour to an hour with it every day as much as possible, and give it enough petting and play.

4. It is necessary to understand the knowledge of cats in advance. In addition to the most basic food, drink and shit, cats will also lose their temper, monthly deworming, annual vaccines, food to eat, things to use, estrus, pregnancy, giving birth to babies, etc. Wait for all aspects of knowledge, instead of getting one home on a whim.

The basic essentials for raising a cat (what to prepare)

  1. Cat litter box (cat toilet), cat litter, cat food, food bowl, water bowl)
  2. Cat litter, you can buy it or not, you don't have to buy it because they will find their favorite place to lie down.
  3. Cat toys (you can play with cat sticks or feathers. Playing with it can also enhance your relationship; you must have a cat scratching board, cats will grind their nails every day, otherwise your furniture will be broken; other cats Toys don't have to be bought, they enjoy themselves)
  4. Nail scissors (for cats, cut nails for cats, cut the tip off about 3mm to reduce the chance of accidental injury)
  5. Pet comb
  6. Cat body wash (absolutely cannot be used for human use, it will stimulate skin diseases)
  7. Towel (recommended deerskin towel, super absorbent and super cheap)
  8. Toothbrush and toothpaste for cats (to prevent dental calculus, otherwise it will be necessary to go to the hospital for surgery)
  9. Cat cage (you can put it in first if you clean or if he makes a mistake)
