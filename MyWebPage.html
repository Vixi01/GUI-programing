Guide to raise your first cat

1. Consider your economic and environmental conditions for raising a cat.

Expenses: Take safe commercial cat dry food as the minimum standard, the monthly cost of cat food + cat litter + necessary medicines should be around $53.

Basic environmental assessment for cat raising: A cat litter box that can be isolated from food and water sources and from where you sleep. And space that can meet for cats to run and climb. If you don't have such conditions, it may be torture for the cat and for you.

2. Have permission of those around you who may be affected, such as landlords, roommates, family members, etc.

3. Small animals need companionship. Although cats are relatively independent, they will be depressed when they are often left alone. Please spend at least half an hour to an hour with it every day as much as possible, and give it enough accompany.

4. It is necessary to understand cats habit in advance. In addition to the most basic food, drink and poop, cats body language, monthly flea treatment, annual vaccines, food to eat, things to use, estrus etc. Spending time to be prepared to raise a cat, instead of getting one home on a whim.

The basic essentials for raising a cat (what to prepare)

  1. Cat litter box (cat toilet), cat litter, cat food, food bowl, water bowl)
  2. Cat bed, better to have one they often use it for the first couple week and they will find their favorite place to lie down.
  3. Cat toys (you can play with cat sticks or feathers. Playing with it can also enhance your relationship; you must have a cat scratching board, cats will grind their nails every day, otherwise your furniture will be broken; small cat toys can also help them to release anxiety when they first move in the new environment.)
  4. Nail scissors for cats, it is necessary to cut nails for cats, cut the tip off about 3 mm to reduce the chance of injury)
  5. Pet comb(comb their hair daily so you don't get their fur all over your furniture.)
  6. Cat body wash (absolutely cannot be used for human use, it will stimulate skin diseases)
  7. Toothbrush and toothpaste for cats (to prevent dental issue, otherwise it will be necessary to go to the hospital for surgery)
  8. Cat cage (Cat cage is often uses when you have to bring them on the car)
